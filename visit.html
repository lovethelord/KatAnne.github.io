TOURS

A tour of the Conservatory will take approximately 90 minutes so please plan to arrive with plenty of time to complete your visit before the closing times noted above. Please note that some areas of the public garden, including the Outdoor Garden and the Tropical Forest Conservatory, may be closed on occasion for private events.
